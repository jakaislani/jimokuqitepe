Sammy Davis Jr.’s Early Life and Career

Sammy Davis Jr. was born on December 8, 1925, in Harlem, New York City. He began his career in entertainment at a very young age, performing with his father and uncle in the Will Mastin Trio. His talent was evident from the start, and he quickly became a popular figure in the entertainment industry.

Breakthrough and Rise to Fame

Davis Jr.’s breakthrough came when he was spotted by Frank Sinatra, who invited him to join the Rat Pack. This association catapulted his career to new heights, and he became one of the most sought-after entertainers of his time.
