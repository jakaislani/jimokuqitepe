    Heavenly Hash Truffle Bars

    Heavenly Hash Truffle Bars are loaded with coconut, pecans, marshmallows and chocolate and topped with a decadent ganache. This incredibly easy treat comes together in minutes and is sure to impress the chocolate lover in your life!

    Course Dessert Cuisine American Keyword heavenly hash, truffle bars Prep Time 15 minutes minutes Total Time 15 minutes minutes Servings 64 servings Calories 132kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
    • 14 oz sweetened condensed milk
    • 1 1/2 cups miniature marshmallows
    • 1 1/2 cups roughly chopped pecans
    • 2 cups unsweetened shredded coconut
    • 1 cup miniature marshmallows

    Topping

    • 10 oz bittersweet chocolate
    • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
    • 1/2 cup finely chopped pecans

    Instructions

    • Line a 9x13 baking dish with parchment paper and set aside.

    • In a large microwave-safe bowl, combine the semi-sweet chocolate chips and sweetened condensed milk.

    • Microwave on high heat in 30 second intervals, stirring in between, until chocolate is melted and mixture is smooth.

    • Stir in marshmallows, pecans, and coconut.

    • Spread mixture into prepared baking dish.

    • Top with another cup of miniature marshmallows and press down gently.

    • Chill until set.

    Topping

    • Place bittersweet chocolate in a heat-proof bowl.

    • Heat whipping cream until scalding hot and pour over the top of the chocolate.

    • Let sit for 5 minutes and then stir until smooth. (If chocolate hasn't melted all the way, heat on high heat in 15 second intervals in the microwave, stirring in between, until chocolate is fully melted.)

    • Pour mixture over the top of the chilled bars and use an offset spatula to spread over the top of the marshmallows.

    • Sprinkle the chopped pecans over the top and chill again until set.

    • Cut into bars and serve.

    • Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
