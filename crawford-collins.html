Crawford Collins- Biography

Crawford Collins is a well-known Canadian Viner and social media star. He is well-known as a cast member of the YouTube video series “The Reality House.” During a question-and-answer session on Ask FM, a local radio station, he once stated that he enjoys skating in his spare time. Also, he enjoys listening to music, particularly Drake’s style. He is a major Selena Gomez fan. His greatest fear is the ocean and sand, thus he never goes to the beach to have fun.

What is the Net Worth of Crawford Collins? Salary, Earnings

Crawford Collins has become a well-known Internet sensation due to his endearing appearance. The young boy has established a good job at a young age and has many more opportunities to do so. According to sources, his net worth is estimated to be over $250,000 USD.

Crawford Collins- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

He was born in Calgary, Canada, on April 15, 1997. His parents’ names are John and Stacey Collins. He has two sisters, Kirsten Collins and Karisma Collins, and one brother named Christian Collins. His brother, Christian, is also a web celebrity, as are his sisters, Kirsten and Karisma.

Collins’ educational background is unknown, however, he, like his elder brother, considers himself home-schooled. His favorite sports are ice hockey and skateboarding.

Crawford Collins- Professional Career

Crawford started his Vine career at a young age. He aspired to be a hockey player as a child, but after being influenced by his older brother, Christian, he turned to the Internet. His siblings were all interested in the sphere of Internet video making. He began making vines and playing computer games with the help of his siblings. Crawford made his maiden vine debut on February 20, 2014, with a video titled “How I Feel When My Phone Dies.” The video went viral and earned a lot of fame in only five months after it was posted. Through this video, he amassed a fan network of over 7,50,000 people at the age of 17.

He later became a YouTube Star and continues to publish vine videos. As a result, he amassed a large number of fan followers, and the majority of his female fans were drawn to him. He frequently collaborated with several other Viners, including Jake Foushee, Brent Rivera, and his siblings. By October 2016, barely more than two years after originally entering the scene, he had amassed over 1.6 million fan followers by publishing approximately 500 vine videos. His YouTube videos and Vines frequently concentrate on current events, adolescent life, or hilarious views on everyday occurrences. Collins constantly attempts to include pop-culture allusions in his skits to make them more relevant. His quick wits and excellent appearance are two characteristics that have contributed to his uniqueness.
