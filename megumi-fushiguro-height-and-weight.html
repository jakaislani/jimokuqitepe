Megumi Fushiguros Height and Weight: Fun Facts and Trivia

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Megumi Fushiguro is one of the fan-favorite characters from Jujutsu Kaisen. His personality and nature help him stand out from the rest of the cast and fans are always ready to know more about him. In the anime Fushiguro’s height seems tall but how tall is he really? And how much does he weigh? In today’s article, we’ll get to know about Megumi Fushiguro’s height, weight, and some other details.

How Tall and Heavy is Megumi Fushiguro?

Megumi Fushiguro stands at a height of 5 feet 9 inches (175 cm). Has a weight of around 137 pounds (62 kg). Megumi possesses a skill known as the Ten Shadows Technique. This enables him to call forth and manipulate shikigami, ethereal spirits, with whom he forms contracts and stores within his shadow. Each shikigami boasts its set of capabilities, which Megumi can merge together to fashion even mightier entities.

Megumi Fushiguro’s Body Statics Compared to Other Jujutsu Sorcerers

Megumi possesses height and weight when compared to the Jujutsu Sorcerers mentioned here. Nonetheless, he maintains fitness enabling him to effectively engage in combat with formidable adversaries.

It’s worth emphasizing that a Jujutsu Sorcerer’s power isn’t solely determined, by their strength and size. Their inherent technique, experience, and overall skill level also significantly contribute to their prowess.

How Powerful is Megumi Fushiguro

Megumi Fushiguro possesses skills as a jujutsu sorcerer despite his age. As a first-year student, at Jujutsu Tech High School he has already achieved the rank of Grade 2 sorcerer. This places him among the up-and-coming sorcerers worldwide.

Megumi’s extraordinary abilities stem from his talent in wielding the Ten Shadows Technique. Through this technique, he can. Manipulate shikigami spirits that he forms contracts, with and stores within his shadow. Each shikigami possesses powers, that Megumi can combine to unleash more formidable entities.

One of Megumi Fushiguro’s techniques is his domain expansion known as Chimera Shadow Garden. By utilizing his Ten Shadows Technique he crafts a perilous realm inhabited by shikigami spirits. Encased within a barrier this domain prevents opponents from escaping its grasp.

Once the domain is activated Megumi gains the ability to summon any of his shikigami allies, including his companion named Max Elephant. Moreover, he can employ the domain to create shikigami or merge existing ones to conjure more awe-inspiring creatures.

Some Fun Facts about Megumi Fushiguro

  • He is 5’9″ tall (175 cm), which is slightly above average height for a Japanese man.The average height for a Japanese male is 5’7″ (172 cm). This is based on a 2022 survey by the Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare of Japan.
  • In the Jujutsu Kaisen series, Megumi has an interest, in nonfiction books as we learn from the Official Fanbook and the first volume of the manga. This detail showcases his inclination towards stimulating pursuits and perfectly complements his demeanor. When at home he prefers to dress finding comfort in attire. It’s delightful to see him enjoying pizza, with Yuji and Nobara in an ambiance, which truly reflects his laid-back hobbies. Nonfiction books serve as his means of gaining knowledge.
  • Many fans of Jujutsu Kaisen are aware that Megumi has a bit of a disconnect, from the Zen’in Clan and their matters. However, some may not realize that the clan is quite interested, in pursuing him because he inherited the Ten Shadows technique, unlike his aunts Maki and Mai Zen’in. What makes him more valuable is not just this inheritance. Also his creation of the Chimera Shadow Garden. In this garden, he can summon the Shikigami known as Eight Handled Sword Divergent Sila Divine General Mahoraga. Despite his importance, Megumi doesn’t feel compelled to play a leading role in the activities of the clan.
  • Megumi is a name that carries the beautiful meaning of “blessing” or “grace.” Although he doesn’t consider himself exceptional his parents named him with the hope that he would one day become a Jujutsu Sorcerer and bring salvation to many. While his unique personality may not immediately align with the interpretation of his name, those who are close, to him perceive him as a source of joy and positivity. Despite his preference, for being addressed as Fushiguro his given name represents resilience and fortitude because he embodies strength within himself.
