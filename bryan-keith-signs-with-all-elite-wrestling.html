Bryan Keith Signs with All Elite Wrestling

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Bryan Keith first made a name for himself in Reality of Wrestling and Ring of Honor. During his time in Reality of Wrestling, Bryan Keith won the ROW Heavyweight Championship one time.

Since Tony Khan acquired ROH, Keith started making sporadic appearances on AEW particularly Collision. Keith has already competed against Roderick Strong, Undisputed Kingdom, and Orange Cassidy.

Tonight on AEW Collision, Keith went one-on-one in a proving grounds match against Eddie Kingston. It was a hard-fought battle between both. Keith came close to beating Kingston. However, it was the Mad King who emerged victorious in the end.

Following the match, Tony Schiavone tried to get an interview with Kingston but he was not interested in doing an interview and asked Tony to interview Bryan Keith instead. Tony then informed Keith and everyone that Keith is All Elite now which even surprised the Bounty Hunter. Following the announcement, Eddie Kingston showed his respect by shaking his hand.

It looks like all the years of hard work of Bryan Keith have finally paid off. It will be interesting to see what Bryan Keith does during this run in AEW. Stay tuned to Ringside News as we bring you live coverage of AEW Collision.
