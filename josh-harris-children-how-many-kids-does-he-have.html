Josh Harris Children: How many kids does he have?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Billionaire Josh Harris is back in the news this with reports claiming that he has led an ownership group in an agreement to purchase the Washington Commanders from Daniel Snyder. In this post, we’ll take a closer look at his personal life, particularly his family and children.

Joshua Jordan Harris, best known as Josh Harris is a prominent American private equity investor and philanthropist, was born in December 1964 in Chevy Chase, Maryland. Harris grew up in the Washington metropolitan area and attended The Field School in Washington D.C. He graduated from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania with a Bachelor of Science degree in economics in 1986, representing the Penn Quakers in collegiate wrestling. Later, he received his MBA from Harvard Business School.

Harris co-founded Apollo Global Management in 1990 with Leon Black and Marc Rowan, where he handled most of its day-to-day operations from its foundation until leaving in 2022 to focus on other ventures. Under his direction, funds managed by Apollo invested $2 billion into LyondellBasell in 2008, and the funds later sold their shares for a profit of $9.6 billion in 2013. In May 2021, Harris resigned from Apollo’s day-to-day operations due to misunderstanding with co-founder Leon Black and his focus on investments in sports teams. He left the company in January 2022.

Harris is well-known for leading investment groups that purchased the Philadelphia 76ers of the National Basketball Association (NBA) and the New Jersey Devils of the National Hockey League (NHL) in the early 2010s. Together with David S. Blitzer, he founded Harris Blitzer Sports & Entertainment (HBSE) in 2017, which manages the 76ers and Devils, among other brands and properties. Harris is also a general partner of Crystal Palace F.C. of the Premier League and a minority owner of the Pittsburgh Steelers of the National Football League.

In addition to his career in private equity and sports management, Harris is also recognized for his philanthropic contributions. He serves on the board of directors of Mount Sinai Hospital, Harvard Business School, and the Wharton School. He is also a member of the Council on Foreign Relations and the Investor Advisory Committee on Financial Markets (IACFM) of the New York Federal Reserve. Harris formerly served on the United States Olympic Committee and the board of Berry Global. In 2018, his family office, HRS Management, bought a majority stake in US Fitness, a fitness club operator.

Josh Harris Children

Josh Harris has five children who go by the names of Bridget, Hannah, Stuart, Thomas, and Pierce Harris. He shares all his children with his wife, Marjorie Harris, but not much is known about his personal family life.

Josh Harris established the Harris Family Charitable Foundation (now known as Harris Philanthropies) in 2014 and has since made several important donations . In 2015, he pledged to donate $3.5 million to the Philadelphia Police Athletic League over five years.
