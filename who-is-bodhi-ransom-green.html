Bodhi Ransom Green: Son of star actors who gets dressed up like a girl

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, May 3, 2024

Behind the glitz and glamour lies a world of intricate family dynamics and personal stories that have shaped the lives of actors. At the heart of this narrative for thespians Megan Fox and actor Brian Austin Green is Bodhi Ransom Green, their second son.

This piece delves into the details of Bodhi Green’s life, his enchanting presence and the significance of his name, and also paints a mental picture of what it feels like for him to be born from the marriage of two prominent Hollywood figures.

Who is Bodhi Ransom Green?

Bodhi Ransom Green is the second son of American actress Megan Fox and actor Brian Austin Green. The young lad bears a striking resemblance to his mother and has garnered the admiration of fans. Megan Fox occasionally shares pictures of her son on Instagram, endearing him to the public and allowing glimpses into his charming personality.

The name “Bodhi” holds significant meaning as it reflects Fox’s personal beliefs and spiritual inclination towards Buddhism. Derived from Sanskrit, “Bodhi” symbolises awakening or enlightenment and embodies the concept of attaining a deeper understanding of the world. Fox’s choice of this name for her son signifies her desire to instil a sense of spiritual curiosity and exploration within him.

Despite the efforts of Green’s parents to shield him from media attention due to his young age, his association with his highly successful parents has garnered him some recognition.

Bodhi Ransom Green age

Green was born on February 14, 2014. He is currently nine years old.

Bodhi Ransom Green’s net worth

Green does not have any major assets of his own as he is still a child, who is solely dependent on his parents.

However, his mother has an estimated net worth of $8 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Bodhi Ransom Green family

Green is the son of Brian Austin Green and Megan Fox, both well-known figures in the entertainment industry. He has two siblings, Noah Shannon Green and Journey River Green. Noah Shannon Green is the oldest of the siblings, while Journey River Green is the youngest.

On his maternal side, Green’s grandmother is Gloria Darlene Cisson, and his grandfather is Franklin Thomas Fox.

Megan Fox (Mother)

Born on May 16, 1986, Megan Denise Fox is an American actress known for her notable contributions to the film industry. She began her acting career in the family film, Holiday in the Sun (2001) and went on to land various roles in both film and television. Notable among her appearances are the teen musical comedy, Confessions of a Teenage Drama Queen (2004) and the ABC sitcom, Hope & Faith (2004–2006).

Fox gained significant recognition with her breakout role as “Mikaela Banes” in the blockbuster action film, Transformers (2007). She reprised the role in its sequel, Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen (2009).

She further showcased her versatility by portraying the title character in the horror comedy, Jennifer’s Body (2009) and starred as “April O’Neil” in the superhero action films, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (2014) and its sequel Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Out of the Shadows (2016).

Additionally, she played “Reagan Lucas” in the fifth and sixth seasons of the Fox sitcom, New Girl (2016–2017).

Fox has graced the pages of several prominent magazines, including Maxim, Rolling Stone and FHM. Her talent and contributions have been acknowledged with various awards, including two Scream Awards and four Teen Choice Awards.

In 2017, Fox came under fire after pictures resurfaced that showed a then four-year-old Bohdi Green and his brother, Noah, dressed up in female apparel while they walked the streets of Los Angeles. According to reports, that was not the first time Fox had let her kids dress up in feminine outfits. While she was accused of emasculating her sons, others showed support for the actress on social media. Subsequently, there were several other occasions where her underage kids were still seen wearing feminine clothes. This stirred discussions and questions about Fox’s parenting.                                      

Brian Austin Green(Father)

Born on July 15, 1973, in Van Nuys, California, Brian Austin Green discovered his passion for performing at a young age. He started his acting career as a child by appearing in commercials and landing roles in television shows like Knots Landing and Small Wonder. These early experiences laid the groundwork for his future success.

Green’s career soared when he took on the role of “David Silver” in the TV series, Beverly Hills, 90210 in 1990. He quickly became a fan favourite and captured the hearts of the show’s dedicated audience. The character’s transformation from an awkward teenager to a confident young adult resonated with viewers and showcased Green’s acting skills.

Additionally, Green has made notable appearances in films such as Domino (2005) and Cross (2011). Although these movies did not achieve mainstream success, Green’s performances received praise from critics who commended his dedication and commitment to his craft.

Green has also appeared in various TV shows, including Freddie, Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles, Anger Management, Desperate Housewives and Rosewood.

Bodhi Ransom Green’s parents

The relationship between Megan Fox and Brian Austin Green was far from stable while they were together. They initially met in 2004 on the set of the TV show, Hope & Faith when Fox was 18 and Green was 30. Despite the notable age difference, they began dating and became a high-profile couple.

Their relationship faced difficulties, leading to a brief break in their engagement in 2009. However, they soon reconciled and eventually got married in a private ceremony in June 2010.

The couple welcomed three children together: Noah Shannon Green in September 2012, Bodhi Ransom Green in February 2014, and Journey River Green in August 2016.

Nevertheless, Fox and Green announced their separation in August 2015 as they revealed that they had been living apart for several months. Despite the separation, they remained committed to co-parenting their children. Fox initiated divorce proceedings, citing irreconcilable differences, but they ultimately decided to give their relationship another chance and stopped divorce proceedings in April 2016.

In May 2020, Fox confirmed her separation from Green after nearly a decade of marriage. This time, the split appeared to be final as Green reportedly filed for divorce in November 2020. The divorce was officially finalised in November 2021.

Despite the end of their romantic relationship, Fox and Green have expressed their dedication to co-parenting and ensuring the well-being of their children.

In June 2020, Megan Fox publicly announced her relationship with singer Machine Gun Kelly shortly after appearing in the music video for his song, Bloody Valentine. On January 12, 2022, Fox announced their engagement.

Brian Austin Green has also moved on. Since October 2020, he has been in a relationship with Australian professional dancer, Sharna Burgess. On February 4, 2022, they shared the news of their expecting their first child together. Their son was born on June 28, 2022.
