Bill Kaulitz is a German singer and songwriter born on September 1, 1989 in Leipzig, Germany. He is best known as the lead singer of the band Tokio Hotel, whose first single "Monsoon" reached #1 in Germany within a month. In 2016, he began performing under the stage name Billy, releasing his debut EP I'm Not OK.
